FROM SIGNAL TO TRADE

Agents that trade on evidence, not vibes.

A research agent scans on-chain wallet flows, social signals, and market feeds around the clock — surfacing a ranked watchlist of tokens worth looking at. It doesn't guess. It scores every candidate across seven dimensions: wallet flow, liquidity quality, volume momentum, social velocity, holder distribution, news catalysts, and contract safety.

When a candidate passes the score threshold, it's handed to a trading agent that backtests it against multiple strategies. Only tokens that clear the regression gate — drawdown limits, walk-forward stability, edge confirmation — get promoted. Everything else is discarded. No overfitting. No hope trades.

Execute — Paper by default, live when you approve

Inference runs through Speakeasy — private models with zero content logging. Your strategies, your prompts, your reasoning never touch a third-party log. Four model routes: deepseek-v3.2 for research, qwen3-thinking for deep reasoning, qwen3-coder for strategy refinement, venice-uncensored for unrestricted analysis. The gateway never sees your wallet keys.

paper mode is the default. Live execution requires your explicit approval. confidence tiers degrade automatically when data quality drops — from normal operation to reweighting to safe mode to full hold. The system protects you from itself. And everything is open source, Apache-2.0. Inspect every strategy, every gate, every adapter.
